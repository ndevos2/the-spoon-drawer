Baked Lemon Pepper Chicken and Rice

Prep time: 20-30 minutes. Cook time: 45 minutes. Total time: around 1:15

Ingredients

3 chicken thighs (skin and bone in)
2 tbsp lemon pepper seasoning (I use Cool Runnings)
Olive oil
1/3 c white wine
2 tbsp unsalted butter
1 chopped onion
4???? garlic cloves
1 1/2 c chicken stock
3/4 c water
Grated parmesan for garnish

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F (175 C).
  2. In a bowl, coat the thighs in the lemon pepper, focusing on the skin (should this really be done now, it just BURNS).
  3. Heat olive oil on medium in oven safe pan, and place thighs skin down. Cook for 3 minutes, flip and cook 3 minutes, then flip back to the skin and cook 2 minutes. Remove to bowl.
  4. Remove chicken from pan, and pour wine to deglaze.
  5. Melt butter in wine, stirring so it doesn't burn.
  6. Cook onion until translucent, then add 4 generous spoons of garlic. Add rice and stir frequently for 4 minutes.
  7. Add chicken stock and water and bring to simmer. Let simmer for 1 minute.
  8. Place chicken on top of rice mixture, pouring any chicken juices back into the pan.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 35 minutes, then remove foil and bake for 10 more minutes (a total time of 45 minutes).
  10. Fluff with spoon and let sit 5-10 minutes - this is about the time it will take to finish your sides and get the meal on the table. Grate parmesan onto rice to garnish.
Notes:
  1. This is a very heavy meal. Very greasy, rich, salty. Very filling, and good when you're craving something heavy. Be warned, it's not for days when you want something light!
