Chickpea and Lentil Curry

Prep time: ???? minutes. Cook time: ??? minutes. Total time: ???

Ingredients

Splash oil
1-2 tsp mustard seed
4 Red thai chillies, rough chopped
4 Garlic cloves, minced
1/2 inch??? ginger, grated
1 Yellow onion, diced
1 tsp Garam masala
3 small bay leaves
2 tbsp Curry poweder
1 tbsp Cumin powder (2 tsp?)
1 tsp chili powder
dash cinnamon
2 tsp corriander (1 tbsp?)
1 tsp tumeric
dash allspice?
1 can diced tomatoes (no salt added)
1/2 c Dried red lentils, rinsed
1 c water
1 boullion cube (I used chicken)
1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
splash coconut milk
Handful cilantro, chopped

  1. Heat oil in pan over medium/medium high heat.
  2. When hot, toast mustard seeds. Once crackling, add fresh chilies.
  3. Once chillies are wilted, add garlic, ginger, and onion. Cook until onions are almsot brown but if you can't wait that's okay too.
  4. Add spices: garam masala, bay leaves, curry powder, ground cumin, chili powder, ground cinnamon, ground coriander, tumeric powder, and ground allspice. Toast until fragrant.
  5. Add tomatoes and stir to combine.
  6. Add water, boullion cube, and lentils. Bring to boil and reduce to simmer. Cook for 20 minutes (???).
  7. Add chickpeas, and cook for another 10 minutes.
  8. Add a splash of coconut milk to achieve desired colour. Stir in chopped cilantro, and cook for 5 more minutes.
  9. Serve over basmati rice, garnished with more chopped cilantro.
Notes:
  1. This can be very spicy, so reduce chiies to preference (or increase!)
  2. Also really good with naan! And helps with spice
