Butternut Squash and Lentil Soup

Prep time: ???? minutes. Cook time: ??? minutes. Total time: ???

Ingredients

1 Butternut squash (mine was 2.88 lbs/1.31 kg), cubed
2 Yellow onions, rough chop
2 c Dry red lentils
2 Bay leaves
4 tbsp Curry Powder
6ish? cloves Garlic, minced
1.5 inches Fresh ginger, grated
Olive oil
7.5 c Water
3 Boullion cubes (I use chicken)

  1. Heat olive oil over medium heat. Cook garlic until fragrant; add curry powder, ginger, stock cubes, and bay leaves.
  2. Add onion, cook until soft, stirring frequently.
  3. Add squash, mix to combine.
  4. Add water. Bring to boil, and reduce to simmer. Simmer for 20 minutes, or until squash is fork-tender.
  5. Move off heat, and wait 10 minutes for the soup to cool.
  6. Transfer soup in batches to stand blender; bend until smooth. Alternatively, use an immersion blender, though it is harder to get a smooth blend this way.
  7. Return to pot, heat to serving temperature, and serve.
Notes:
  1. Some yummy toppings include toasted pumpkin seeds, a swirl of coconut milk, or a drizzle of tahini.
