Tarkha Daal

Prep time: ??? minutes. Cook time: 60 minutes. Total time: ???

Ingredients

2 c red lentils, rinsed
6 c water
2 tsp tumeric
2 tsp chili powder
2 tsp ground coriander
1 tbsp garam masala
2 tsp cumin + garnish
1 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp??? ground fenugreek
Salt to taste
1/2 c butter
1 bulb garlic, minced
Handful chopped cilantro + garnish

  1. Rinse lentils until water runs clear.
  2. Add lentils to 6 cups boiling water in a large saucepan.
  3. Boil the lentils for about 10 minutes, skimming any foam produced during this time.
  4. Add spices and reduce heat to simmer.
  5. Simmer, covered, for 45 minutes, stirring every 5-10 minutes to prevent sticking.
  6. Continue simmering, uncovered, to reduce to desired consistency. Add more water if necessary.
  7. In a pan, melt the butter on medium/medium low heat and add the garlic. Cook, stirring frequently, until the garlic is lightly toasted and the butter browned.
  8. Add butter-garlic mix to the lentils, and stir to fullt incorportate.
  9. Add some cumin and cilantro, and serve over basmati rice.
