Crockpot Peameal Bacon Potato Chowder

Prep time: ?? minutes. Cook time: 4-6 hours. Total time: ??

Ingredients

1 lb peameal bacon
2 tbsp butter
2 leeks, sliced and rinsed
1 large yellow onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
6 cups boiling potatoes (I use Yukon Gold), peeled and diced
2 bay leaves
1 tsp dried thyme
1 tsp ground black pepper
1/2 tsp salt (I did 40 turns of a salt grinder)
5 cups stock (chicken or vegetable, or a combination. I did 2c Chicken, 3c Veg)
1 can evaporated milk
1 cup half-and-half

  1. Melt butter in a pan on medium heat. Add sliced leek, diced onion, and minced garlic. Cook until translucent, about 4 minutes (??)
  2. Add leek mixture, potatoes, stock, bay leaves, thyme, and salt and pepper to the crockpot. Cook on low for 6 hours or high for 4 hours.
  3. An hour before the soup is done, cook pemeal bacon in the oven for 45-60 minutes at 375 F. Allow to cool and dice.
  4. Remove 2 cups of potatoes and the bay leaves, and use an immersion blender to blend until smooth.
  5. Stir in milks.
  6. Add in diced peameal bacon and reserved potatoes.
  7. Serve.
Notes:
  • Using condensed milk isn't terrible, but it's certainly a different flavour.
  • This is a peppery, warm soup!
  • Try serving with breads and cheeses, with more bacon for garnish.
  • Can use a fresh sprig of thyme - remove with the bay leaves.
  • Recipe based on the Peameal Bacon Potato Chowder recipe shared by Ontario Pork: Slow Cooker Peameal and Potato Chowder by Tara Ballantyne
