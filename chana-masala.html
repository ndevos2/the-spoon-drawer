Quick Chana Masala

Prep time: ?? minutes. Cook time: ?? minutes. Total time: ??

Ingredients

2 cans chickpeas (18oz?)
1 can diced tomatoes (26 oz)
Olive oil
3?? clove??? minced garlic
1/2 tsp ground ginger
2 tsp Garam Masala
1/2 large red onion, diced
1 green chili, sliced finely
1 tsp Amchur powder (dried mango powder)
1 tbsp sugar
water as needed
fresh cilantro, chopped

  1. Heat 2 tsp olive oil in a sauce pot.
  2. Add garam masala and ginger powder; roast until fragrant.
  3. Add garlic, fry.
  4. Add onions and cook, stirring frequently, until translucent and slightly brown.
  5. Add tomatoes and cook, continuing to stir frequenctly, until the tomatoes are soft and most of the liquid has evaporated.
  6. Add water as necessary; using an immersion blender, blend to desired blended-ness.
  7. Add both cans of drained chickpeas.
  8. Simmer for 20 minutes or so, adding water to reach desired consistency.
  9. Add Amchur powder, sugar, and salt to taste.
  10. Stir in fresh cilantro.
  11. Serve on Basmati rice
Notes:
  1. Can adjust spiciness level by adding more chilies. Momma likes it with no more than one. Though the tingle could have been the tomato acid...
